Empress Deck

Balance

For 30 secs, apply an additional doom card for every skill used.

Corrosion

Apply a debuff with a 30% chance of increased damage on an enemy by 10%, for 5 seconds.

Cull

Increase your critical hit chance by 100% and critical hit damage by 50%.

Ghost

For 16 seconds increases movement speed by 20% and allows you to pass through enemies.

Judgement

For 4 secs, whenever you use a Ruin Skill, it will always hit as if the enemies have 4 doom cards on them.

Mayhem

For 30 secs when you attack, increase your attack speed by 3% per attack up to 15%.

Three-Heads

Allow you to throw 3 cards in a cone instead of 1 while auto-attacking dealing more damage.

Moon

For 30 secs, cooldown -20%.

Royal

Instantly generates two Tarot cards.

Star

Recover 50% of all mana and increases mana regen rate by 20% for 20 secs.

Twisted Fate

For 4 secs, increase your damage by 40% or remain unchanged.

Wheel of Fortune

The cooldown of the next skill is reduced by 100% including your awakening.

Clown

Re-acquire the most recently used card.

Joy

Instantly reduce the remaining cooldown time of a skill other than awakening, mover, and wake-up call by 15%, or reduce the cooldown time by 30.0% immediately, with a 50.0% probability.

Emperor Deck

The emperor deck adds the Emperor card and replaces balance and judgement with Droplet and Lord. Seen Below

Emperor

Special card that unlocks only with the class engraving Emperor's Edict. Deal damage within 14m.

Droplet

Crit rate of all yellow skills is increased by 20% for 10 secs.

Lord

Damage of all yellow skills is increased by 50% for 4 secs.
